STEPS THAT YOU NEED TO TAKE IN ORDER TO HAVE A BETTER LIFE

Achieving a better life is something that many people aspire to today. This is because it brings financial security, personal fulfillment, and social recognition — ultimately leading to a happier and more comfortable life. Success can be defined as the achievement of meaningful goals, the fulfillment of desires, and the realization of one’s purpose. It is important because it gives people a clear direction and helps them find a true sense of purpose in life.

However, striving for a meaningful and successful life is never easy, especially for those just starting out. Many people have no clear idea of how to begin their journey or what steps to take. In this post, you will learn some practical steps that you need to take in order to have better way of life.

WHY EVERYBODY WANTS TO BE SUCCESSFUL IN LIFE?

Success helps ensure financial security by creating a stable foundation of resources, knowledge, and habits that protect against uncertainty and support long-term growth. True financial success — defined as having enough income and wealth to sustain an ideal life — goes beyond high earnings to include the wisdom to manage money effectively. When it comes to achieving personally fulfilling goals, success acts as a powerful reinforcing mechanism: it builds confidence, provides resources, and creates momentum. Achieving small, intentional wins — often seen as forms of success — fuels intrinsic motivation and validates the effort invested, making further, larger goals more attainable and rewarding. Additionally, success serves as a key catalyst for social recognition, as it acts as tangible proof of competence, value, and effort, addressing the deep‑seated human need for appreciation.

WHY IT'S HARD FOR EVERYONE TO ACCOMPLISH ANY GOALS FROM THE BEGINNING?

Why do most people get stuck or struggle right from the start? The truth is, it happens because they do not know the very first step they need to take. You cannot reach your destination if you don't know the process, after all, success is built step by step.

Many people struggle to figure out how to improve their lives from the very start. Everyone invests their time, effort, and energy looking for answers — searching online and even reading various articles. Yet in the end, they often find nothing, leaving them clueless and confused. If you are just starting out on your journey and have no experience with achieving success, that lack of guidance can definitely become a major problem. How can you move forward if you do not even know the first step to take? This is one of the key reasons why so many people find it difficult to reach their goals.

Here's what happens: not knowing the first step on how to achieve any goals can lead to a feeling of frustration and draining your energy. It can create a psychological paralysis where the desire for progress is blocked by uncertainty, fear and overwhelming complexity. And it can also trigger a fear of failure where you dread wasting your time, energy and resources in the wrong action. Without a clear starting point, you cannot build the initial momentum needed to keep going. In some cases most people are stuck just thinking about their dreams and even desire in life for days, weeks and even several months and nothing happens.

Pursuing success in life is never easy, especially for those who have no prior experience. Skipping the very first step can easily lead to several mistakes or even failure. So let's get to it, here are some steps that you need to take to have a better life:

PUT GOD FIRST WHEN IT COMES TO BUILDING A BETTER LIFE

If you are starting your journey toward success in life, the first thing you should do is seek guidance from the Almighty above. This is because God gives us direction and purpose. Putting God first and holding fast to your faith is truly one of the strongest foundations you can have when building a better life.

When you put God first, you know you are not walking this path alone. You trust that He has a plan for you and that He will guide your every step. Instead of worrying about where your life is heading, you gain peace of mind knowing you are on the right course. Faith brings you clarity and a clear sense of direction — just as a compass guides a ship, faith guides your life. Trusting in God also gives you strength when times get tough. Always remember that life is not always easy; there will be problems, failures, and moments when you feel exhausted or want to give up. Faith helps you stand firm, reminding you that you do not have to face everything on your own. It fills you with hope, assuring you that your present situation is not permanent and that better days are yet to come.

Turning to God also helps free you from fear and anxiety. Many people struggle to move forward because they are afraid of failure, of loss, or of what lies ahead. But when you trust in God, you learn to let go of fear. You know that He is in control and that He will take care of all things. When you stop worrying constantly, you are able to think more clearly, work more effectively, and build your life with confidence. Faith also teaches you to wait patiently and trust the process. You understand that everything happens in its own perfect time. This patience keeps you from making mistakes caused by rushing, and helps you build something solid and lasting.

THE FIRST KEY TO A BETTER LIFE IS BEING WELL EDUCATED

To achieve success in life, the first step you need to take is to become well‑educated. Many people mistakenly believe that being well‑educated simply means holding advanced degrees or being naturally intelligent — but this view is completely wrong. In reality, being well‑educated means having the ability to understand the world around you and the capacity to think critically and independently.

Success is often linked to formal education, but the truth is, it is not limited to those who hold a master’s degree or even a college diploma. While education can provide structure, knowledge, and opportunities, it is not the only path to achieving meaningful success in life. One of the main reasons success is possible without a formal degree is easy access to information. Today, knowledge is no longer confined to classrooms. Through the internet, books, and real‑world experiences, anyone can learn valuable and practical skills. Self‑education allows people to focus directly on what matters most to their goals — whether that is business, technology, communication, or creative work. Many highly successful individuals are self‑taught, proving that curiosity and initiative can be even more powerful than formal qualifications.

Some people are unable to complete or pursue a college degree due to a lack of financial resources. Because of that, many may feel they have no chance of building a better life. But let me tell you this — that belief is not entirely true. What truly matters today is having the ability to understand the world around you. Which means that you do not need to be the smartest person to achieve a meaningful and successful life.

UNDERSTANDING THE WORLD IS CRUCIAL FOR ACHIEVING GOALS

Why is understanding the world crucial for achieving your goals? It is because it provides the context, resources, and foresight you need to navigate obstacles and recognize opportunities. It helps you create realistic, effective plans and develop relevant skills. When you understand how the world works, how economies function, and what people truly need, you can spot opportunities where others only see problems. By perceiving your surroundings accurately, you are able to move beyond abstract dreams and take concrete, effective action.

Our surroundings present both advantages and challenges. Being aware of our environment helps us identify valuable social networks, emerging trends, and useful technologies that can act as catalysts for success. It also enables informed decision-making: the knowledge you gain from observing the world allows you to weigh the pros and cons of different options based on facts and evidence, rather than guesswork. Understanding how the world works also helps you take calculated risks — being aware of potential hazards, whether social, political, or physical, lets you take proactive steps to avoid or overcome difficulties before they arise. Additionally, this understanding builds persistence and resilience. When you see the bigger picture, you are able to view failure as a learning opportunity rather than an obstacle.

MAKING A PLAN CAN LEAD TO GREATER SUCCESS

Understanding how the world works helps everyone create effective plans. Making a plan is just like having a reliable map and weather forecast before starting a journey — you cannot prepare properly if you do not know what the terrain looks like or what challenges lie ahead. This is why understanding the world around you is so important when building a better life.

Having a clear plan also helps you avoid unnecessary mistakes. When you understand history, how businesses operate, or how people behave, you become aware of common reasons why things fail. Someone who understands these patterns follows proven paths and avoids dangerous pitfalls. This allows you to build plans based on reality rather than just wishful thinking. Additionally, understanding the world helps you anticipate future difficulties or challenges, so you can prepare backup strategies. If things do not go as expected, you will be ready and able to respond because you have already considered such possibilities. With a solid plan, you can also set meaningful, realistic goals: by observing the world, you can identify what people need, what trends are emerging, and where the best opportunities lie. It also helps you make the best use of available resources — knowing exactly what tools, funding, or connections are within reach.

BUILDING SUCCESS IS ABOUT FINDING OUT HOW THINGS WORK IN LIFE

Having a deep understanding of how things work in life can lead you to a much better way of living. To some people, this may sound like just a simple phrase that does not deserve serious attention. However, for others, it carries profound meaning — especially when it comes to achieving true success and greatness in life.

Everyone has their own views about life — especially regarding what it means and how it works. There is nothing wrong with that. What truly matters, however, is being aware of all possible outcomes of your beliefs and actions, whether positive or negative. Understanding how life works helps you develop the ability to assess situations clearly, appreciate different perspectives, and recognize how actions lead to specific consequences. Life is governed by systems, patterns, and cause‑and‑effect relationships. Those who understand this principle generally make better choices, because they know that what they do today will shape their future tomorrow.

When you understand the principles behind how things work, you realize that every action brings its own consequence. For instance, you recognise that investing time in learning leads to greater knowledge and new opportunities — and this awareness motivates you to study or work hard rather than put things off. Life often follows repeating patterns, and understanding these cycles helps you anticipate what may happen next. In the same way, understanding how the human mind works helps you manage your own emotions. This is perhaps the most valuable insight of all, because it allows you to truly understand both yourself and others. It enables you to stop making decisions driven by fear, greed, or impulse. When you understand why people behave the way they do, you gain a clear advantage in relationships, career growth, and negotiations.

In short, understanding life turns you from someone who is just "surviving" and reacting to events, into someone who is "thriving" and actively designing their future.

CURIOSITY IS ESSENTIAL FOR BUILDING A BETTER LIFE

Another essential element in building a better life is curiosity. It is the natural desire and ability to learn, explore, and understand how things work — the inner drive that motivates people to ask questions, seek knowledge, and discover new ideas. Curiosity is vital for achieving success because it helps you develop a deeper understanding of both yourself and the world around you. This kind of mindset encourages continuous learning, which in turn leads to better decision‑making and ongoing personal growth.

When you understand how the world works, you naturally develop curiosity by asking meaningful questions about the things around you. This quality is essential for success because it sparks creativity and strengthens problem‑solving skills. When you are curious, you are more willing to experiment, explore different possibilities, and think critically. This mindset often leads to fresh ideas, innovations, and effective solutions to challenges. In every area of life — whether in education, career, or personal growth — curiosity serves as a powerful driver of progress and development.

Curiosity also helps you stay motivated and engaged with life. When you are eager to learn and discover new things, life feels more exciting and meaningful. Instead of feeling trapped in routine, a curious person sees every situation as a chance to gain new knowledge. This attitude builds greater confidence, adaptability, and resilience when facing difficulties.

BE A GOOD THINKER TO ACHIEVE A BETTER QUALITY OF LIFE

A good thinker is not simply someone who is intelligent or has a high IQ — it is someone who knows how to think, not just what to think. And this kind of ability is essential for achieving true greatness in life.

Someone who thinks well questions everything. They do not simply accept what others say — instead, they always ask “why” and “how”. Asking these two questions acts as the driving force behind critical thinking and personal growth. This is because they push you to move from a state of passive existence to one of active mastery. Passive existence is like being a leaf floating on water: you simply go with the flow, accept things as they are, and merely react to whatever happens to you. In contrast, active mastery means taking charge and creating your own reality. It involves understanding how things work, changing what does not serve you or work well, and planning and acting with clear intention. This is one of the key secrets to transforming your life for the better.

Asking “why” wakes up your mind. For example, if you feel poor, unhappy, or stuck in life, asking “why” helps you get to the bottom of the situation. You might ask: Why am I struggling financially? Why do I feel stuck? The answers could be because of certain habits, a lack of knowledge, or the environment you are in. This kind of questioning stops you from blaming others and helps you identify the real root of the problem.
